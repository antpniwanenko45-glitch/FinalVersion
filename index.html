⚠️ Study Instructions
  1. After clicking Start, a chat interface with an AI assistant will open.
  2. Imagine you want to find new sneakers for the summer.
  3. Type a message asking the AI for recommendations for shoes that would suit you best.
  4. Press the green button to send your message.
  5. Carefully read the response provided by the AI.
  6. Then press the green button again to open the questionnaire and answer the questions.

By clicking Start, you agree to participate in this study.
You can withdraw at any time.
